Maintenance History

10/28/25:

The conveyor was not running.  Discovered that the left edge of the belt had torn and had jammed into the left-side tracker.  A new belt and tracker were ordered.

11/05/25:

The new conveyor belt was installed.  Tension and alignment checked as described in the instruction manual.  The spare/new trackers were placed within the cabinet of the sander.  Drum alignment to the table was checked and adjusted as well.
